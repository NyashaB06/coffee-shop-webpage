About Us

Mountain Brew is nestled in the picturesque hills of Shimla, offering a tranquil escape from the busy world. With views of the majestic Himalayas, our coffee shop is the perfect place to unwind, whether you're enjoying a cup indoors or outside with the crisp mountain air. We source our beans locally from the best high-altitude farms, ensuring every cup delivers a fresh and flavorful experience.
